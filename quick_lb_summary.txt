WHY THIS STRUCTURE?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. FAIRNESS: Teams that lose early get more comeback chances
   - WR1 loser → 7 possible LB matches
   - WR5 loser → 1 possible LB match

2. BALANCE: Alternating WB drops with LB-only rounds prevents 
   overcrowding and ensures competitive matches

3. CHALLONGE STANDARD: This exact structure matches Challonge's 
   tournament system used worldwide
